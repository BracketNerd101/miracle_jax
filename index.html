Welcome

Welcome to Jax Roller Hockey. This is the official website for the inline hockey organization of Jacksonville Florida. Here you can find out about the league, schedules important links and anything you might want to know about our organization.

Playing Options

We offer a full season for players and goalies. On Saturdays we practice for all skill levels. During the off season we play pick up hockey. During pick up out skaters are charged $5.00 and Goalies play free.

Gear you will need to play

A variety of gear and hockey equipment is needed to play. Read below to findout what you need to play.

Skaters Need...

CSA approved helmet with cage
Elbow pads, Gloves,Hockey Stick
Ice hockey pants or inline hockey pants (with or without girdle)
Shin pads, Athletic cup
Inline hockey skate

Goalies Need...

CSA approved goalie helmet and cage
Upper body protector, Throat guard
Catcher, Blocker, Throat guard
Hockey pants, Girdle/Protective Cup
Goalie pads, Goalie stick, Roller skates
