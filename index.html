Jax Roller hockey

Welcome to Jax Roller Hockey. This is the official website for the inline hockey organization of Jacksonville Florida. Here you can find out about the league, schedules important links and anything you might want to know about our organization.

We offer a full season for players and goalies. On Saturdays we practice for all skill levels. During the off season we play pick up hockey. During pick up out skaters are charged $5.00 and Goalies play free.

Are you ready to play?!

A variety of gear and hockey equipment is needed to play. Read below to findout what you need to play

Goalies Need:

CSA approved helmet with cage
Elbow pads, Gloves,Hockey Stick
Ice hockey pants or inline hockey pants (with or without girdle)
Shin pads, Athletic cup
Inline hockey skate

Adult Hockey

We play Tuesdays at 8pm and 9pm are the scheduled games. We also offer drop in hockey where players pay 5$ at the door and Goalies play free. These drop in hockey days are usually scheduled and announced on our facebook page. Facebook Roller Hockey

Youth Hockey

Kids play Monday and Tuesday at two different times. 6pm , 7pm are the scheduled games. Please follow us on facebook for announcements, events and schedule changes. Facebook Roller Hockey

Clinics and Saturday Practices

We offer different drills, skating exercises and opportunities to scrimmage. Check facebook for updates for saturday clinics. Times may vary but expect to wake up before 6am.
